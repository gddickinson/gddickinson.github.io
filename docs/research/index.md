# Research Overview

My research spans calcium signaling, mechanotransduction, super-resolution microscopy, and nucleic acid nanotechnology. Work across multiple labs has contributed to understanding cellular signaling mechanisms from the molecular to systems level.

### Previous Research

#### Single-Molecule Calcium Imaging  
**Parker Lab, UC Irvine (2010-2016)**

Pioneered optical patch-clamp techniques to study IP3 receptor channel dynamics during calcium puffs at single-channel resolution.

[Details →](parker-lab.md)

#### Lysosomal Calcium Signaling  
**Patel Lab, University College London (2002-2010)**

Characterized NAADP receptors and lysosomal calcium release mechanisms in neurons and sea urchin eggs.

[Details →](patel-lab.md)

#### Plant Calcium Signaling  
**Sanders Lab, University of York (1998-2002)**

PhD research identifying and characterizing NAADP-induced calcium release from the endoplasmic reticulum in higher plants.

[Details →](sanders-lab.md)

## Techniques & Methods

### Microscopy
- Total Internal Reflection Fluorescence (TIRF)
- DNA-PAINT super-resolution imaging
- STORM (Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy)
- Confocal and light-sheet microscopy
- Optical patch-clamp (single-channel calcium imaging)

### Computational Methods
- Custom image analysis software development
- Machine learning for image classification
- Stochastic modeling of ion channel dynamics
- Big data processing (cloud and local)
- Geospatial data analysis

### Molecular Biology
- DNA origami assembly
- Cell culture and transfection
- Fluorescent probe development
- PCR assay development
- Protein expression and purification
